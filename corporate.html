What is Femme-Unity, and why would people want to be a part of it?

Femme Unity offer yoga classes designed specifically for middle-aged working single moms in the Netherlands. The classes provide an opportunity for moms to take a break for their busy schedules, in addition to the classes the women attending have an opportunity to socialize and network with like-minded women. To ensure that the classes are accessible to as many moms as possible, the program offers flexible scheduling and payment options. Moms can choose to attend individual classes or sign up for a package of classes at a discounted rate. A small percentage of the subscription fees are set aside for scholarships for moms who may not be able to afford the full costs of the program, this way every mom is able to join Femme Unity.

Femme Unity provides a support system that some single moms may miss; it gives them the opportunity to take a break and get a sense of rest and tranquility. Using the magic of yoga, it allows them to improve their physical and mental health and their overall well-being while forming connections with other single moms.

Font choices

For our website we decided to use two different fonts. For the text part of our website, we decided to go with the font "Times New Roman", as it is a classic and versatile font that has been widely used in digital media for decades. The font is easy to read and has a professional and authoritative feel. Additionally, the style adds calmth to the overall website as it is not too destracting for the viewer. On the other hand, since our general font is basic and therefore not distracting, we decided to add a more playful font for our headings. For this font we used "Chicle", a modern google font that is perfect for adding a fun and whimsical touch to designs. It is a script font with flowing, cursive-like letterforms that give it a unique and distinctive look. Its informal and friendly style makes it inviting for the viewer and gives a sense of creativity and approachability.


User interface patterns

Femme Unity makes use of several interface patterns. Though we wanted to keep it simple and manageable for our target audience, we did want to include multiple elements, such as cards and carousels. For our values, team, and services-read more sections we have decided to use the card interface pattern, whereas we used a carousel for our services-main section. We wanted to minimize the information given to the visitor, as to why we have decided to "hide" the services-read more card section by creating a href to the actual page. Secondly, we wanted to give the viewer an idea of our values by keeping it simple and keep them from being distracted. This is why we made a card pattern for our values with just our values written out, in case the visitor would like to see what Femme Unity values and stands for. Additionally, we used cards for our "teams" section where we yet again kept it minimalistic but also added pictures of the team, so that the visitor is able to imagine a face behind the product as well as feel invited.


The structure of the navigation and content

In terms of the navigation and content of the Femme Unity website, it was important to us to not cause information overload for the visitors. Therefore, we made sure to minimalize the information at first glance, and section the content into several pages (about, values, services, etc.). When it comes to our services, we designed our website so that the visitor was first able to see what kind of classes we offer and therefore our range, and if-and-when interested in a specific class, they are able to click on the "read more" button to find out more about the class. This way we will not overwhelm our visitors with information, but we will still be able to provide them our options. Additionally, we created some cards with interesting information, which we made sure is spread equally over the pages and are all on the same level. This was important to us, as we did not want to have any distractions on the website for our visitors.

Testing Report

Testing goals

With this usability testing report, we are trying to find out several things:

Firstly, we would like to find out if the users think our website is clear and easy to navigate through. Meaning, Can they find the information that they want to find on our website, and can they do what they intended to do when getting on the website. So they have to evaluate the navigation and organization of our website. Next to that, we would like to find out if the users face errors or bugs on the website that we may have overseen. They will have to identify usability issues.

Lastly, with this usability testing we can find out whether the users may face some problems or have any other additional tips and ideas that we can use to improve our website. This can be either technical or design aspects.


Testing methods

We have decided to use the same people we used for our solution interview because they are part of our target group (middle aged women).

We decided to use 1 tester per team member, so for us we are 4 members so we will do the user testing with 4 participants

The participant will be making use of a laptop that is provided to them by us. This will be done in person and the screen will be recorded with a voice over or via a video. The whole webiste of Femme-Unity will be used as the prototype, except for the corporate page.

We will use the Think Aloud Protocol: where we will ask all participants to vocalize their thoughts as they are interacting with the website.

Testing results

After analyzing the feedback from users, it can be concluded that the UI and UX of the website have both positive and negative aspects. The visually appealing about page, consistent use of fonts, and easy navigation are some of the positive aspects of the website. The personal touch given to the homepage, such as the inclusion of a video, also makes for a good user experience.

Moreover, the highly visually appealing design of the website gives participants a relaxed and calm feeling. Users also appreciated the video on the home page and the carousel on the "services" page, which helped to showcase the offerings of the business. Additionally, the use of Cards on the "team" page was well-received, as it provided a quick overview of the team members.

One of the most appreciated aspects of the website was the use of fitting pictures and colors that catered to the target audience. The consistent color use was visually pleasing, and the pictures used were well-suited to the website's theme.

However, there were some areas of the website that could be improved. Users pointed out that some alignments were not perfect, and the font usage could be improved, especially in non-heading texts. The users also noted that some details were missing on the team page, such as their roles. Therefore, it is recommended to clarify the roles of the team members and possibly attach their contact details to their Cards.

Furthermore, while the website is overall very well-designed for a prototype, it was noted that there were some small mistakes that could be corrected, such as missing quotes or imperfect alignment of text and pictures.

In conclusion, the feedback from users has highlighted both the positive and negative aspects of the UI and UX of the website. By incorporating the suggested improvements, the website can be made even better to provide a great user experience to its visitors.

Learning Points

Reflection

One main reflection point for us was that Instagram posts weren’t getting as much interaction as we thought they would. On the first post we got around 17 likes which is more than we expected but we only got 4 comments. Once we started posting more and figuring out what our target audience enjoyed such as our logo and the wellness tips, we managed to quickly turn this around and increase the interaction on our posts. On one of our posts, we even managed to get around 17 comments, almost as much as it had likes. We also noticed that posting regularly worked to increase our reach and follower’s base. The more we posted the more followers we got. Eventually people even started to respond to our posts. Here we could also see what our followers where thinking about our brand and the classes. We noticed that the content of the posts we make have a significant relation to the amount of reach it gets. The post where we shared tips on wellness and mindfulness got more interaction. Therefore, we decided to post 5 new wellness tips and just as predicted it got more interaction just like the first post with tips. It did better than 85% of our other posts according to Instagram.

We also noticed that next to the post with the tips the posts with the most likes and comments was the one of our logos where we announced our website release date. That was something we did not expect in terms of executing our media plan.

Upon reflection we noticed that although our posts reach a lot of accounts it primarily reaches non followers, but the engaged audience are mostly followers.

For our initial idea we wanted to have a Facebook page too. For our Facebook page we thought it would be convenient to use a function in Facebook that would allow us to put a timer on posts so that we would not have to post it ourselves. This is an experience we learned from because we later noticed Facebook did not post these because of an internal error. This set us back because we then did not make the posts according to the plan, but it was a valuable learning moment for the future to always check if something is posted/communicated successfully. We also concluded that our target audience uses Instagram more, so we chose to focus on that and make sure we really covered that platform well. Therefore, we decided to scrap Facebook from our media plan for now and add more social media platforms in the future to increase our reach.

Identified learning points
  • The content of the posts we make have a significant relation to the amount of reach it gets.

  • The logo of femme unity does well within our audience.
  • We need to figure out how to make non followers into followers after they have been reached.
  • Special functions for posting cannot be relied on entirely, and need to be dubble checked.

Revenue streams

  • Single class fee: We charge a fee for each individual yoga class, and it is set at €12.50. This is a usage fee-based revenue model.
  • Subscription: the business offers a discounted rate for those who purchase a package deal of classes in advance. For instance, 10 yoga classes are offered for €115, providing a discount compared to paying for each individual class. This is a subscription-based revenue model.
  • Merchandise: ones the business is rising in popularity we could sell branded merchandise, such as yoga mats, tote bags or t-shirts to generate additional revenue. This is an asset-sale based subscription model since Femme-Unity would sell ownership to a physical product.
  • Renting out our space: with advertising we want to let people know that it is possible to rent our space for a couple of hours, so people could host their events here. This would be a renting-based revenue model, since we would temporarily grant someone the exclusive right to use our space.
  • Teacher training programs & workshop: we offer trainings and workshops to middle aged women who might be interested to become a yoga teacher. Since they must pay for the workshop and training this could be an additional revenue stream. This is a usage fee-based revenue model.

Overall, the revenue model for the business is based on providing yoga classes for middle-aged single moms in the Netherlands and charging fees for attending these classes. The business generates revenue from multiple streams such as single class fees, package deals, scholarship fees and merchandise. (Connor, 2019; Finch, 2022)

Services/products

Femme-Unity offers yoga classes followed by a support group to provide middle-aged single moms a much-needed break from their busy day-to-day lives and the opportunity to create a supporting network. This unique combination of yoga and support group distinguishes Femme-Unity from other yoga studios and support groups since it fulfills the specific needs and wishes from this target groups. By offering a safe space where the women can prioritize self-care and connection with others who share similar experiences, Femme-Unity offers a valuable and unique service. The fit between the service that Femme-Unity offers, and the developed brand identify is strong. Femme-Unity’s brand identify is centered around creating a save and inclusive space where women feel welcomed and supported. Conclusion of the interviews

Problem interviews

After conducting interviews, it is clear that most women are perfectly happy being single and are not actively looking for a romantic partner. There are many different reasons for this, like changes in the dating world or personal experiences with relationships, and some women just do not feel like pursuing a relationship currently. However, it is clear from the interviews that all the women miss some kind of support system in any way. This could mean getting emotional support, or even professional support. They mentioned that being single is fulfilling to them, but they did express a desire for more community events or social activities geared towards middle aged single, which could provide an opportunity to meet like-minded individuals in a low-pressure setting.

Conclusion Interviews

After conducting the solution interviews, we discovered some valuable insights. The women who were interviewed, were excited about the prices and loved the idea of combining yoga classes with a support group. However, they did mention that it maybe should be another word instead of support group. They appreciated the affordability of the program, which could eventually in the future could be a little bit more expensive. During the interviews, the women expressed interest in having the option to form groups with the same women each time they attended the support group. They believed that seeing familiar faces would build trust and allow them to form deeper connections with each other over time. Additionally, some women didn’t feel the need to have a support group, but still liked the idea of having yoga classes with the same age group. Because this way they are not confronted with younger people, who might be able to do more, and this would make them feel more confident and comfortable during the yoga classes. Therefore, in the future we will consider offering separate options for the support groups and yoga classes to accommodate different preferences. They also spoke about the possibility of having a place where there children be taken care of while they are having their yoga classes, because this way it makes it easier for them to attend the yoga classes. Overall, the interviews showed that there is a demand for a women’s support group and yoga business, but it’s also important to consider the needs and preferences of the target audience to create a successful engaging program.

Demo/ prototype

Femme-Unity offers yoga classes in the Netherlands, especially for middle-aged single moms. The classes are designed to provide moms with a much-needed break from their hectic schedules, and to offer them an opportunity to socialize, and creating a supporting network with like-minded women. Since most moms have a lot on their plate, we will offer flexible scheduling options. Moms can choose to attend individual classes for €20,- or purchase a package of 10 classes at discounted rate of €115. This way, they can fit it in their busy schedules how they like it without any stress our pressure. Since the costs of the program may be a barrier for some, we made it as accessible as possible, hence why the low price per class. But we will also set aside a small percentage of the fees for scholarships for moms who may not be able to afford the costs of the program. This way, every mom has the opportunity to join Femme-Unity and take part in the program regardless of their financial situation. Our classes are led by an experienced yoga instructress, who understands the needs of middle-aged single moms since the has experience with teaching yoga, coaching single women and is a part of the target group herself. Within the classes we focus on relaxation, mindfulness, and self-care to help the moms find a balance and clarity amidst the chaos of their life. At Femme-Unity we are committed to create a supportive and inclusive community for singe middle aged moms. We believe that by coming together and taking care of ourselves, we can empower ourselves and one another to live happier and healthier lives.
