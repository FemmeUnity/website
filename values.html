Fostering a sense of community

At Femme Unity, fostering a sense of community is at the heart of who we are and what we stand for. We believe that bringing single mothers together is vital for empowering women. We are committed to creating a safe space where everyone feels welcomed, heard, and supported. We believe that by building a strong community, we can create a network of support and inspiration for single women on their challenging and magical journey of motherhood. Through our yoga classes, we strive to cultivate meaningful connections among our community members. We believe that every individual has a unique perspective and contribution to offer. Join us at Femme Unity and be part of a community that uplifts, empowers, and inspires.

Providing a safe space

A crucial brand value for Femme Unity is providing a safe space. We believe that every woman, every mother, deserves to have a space where they feel supported, respected, and heard. We want to create an environment where women can freely express themselves and share their personal stories. Our commitment to providing a safe space goes beyond physical safety. We care about emotional well-being and strive to create an environment of empathy and mutual respect. Every woman, every mother, can thrive in this safe space, regardless of their background, identity, or circumstances.

Prioritizing
self-care

At Femme Unity, we believe that taking time to care for oneself i s essential for maintaining physical, emotional, and mental well-being. As such, we want to inspire single mothers to integrate self-care as part of their daily routine. We offer self-care in the form of our yoga classes and support groups, this way, we allow our members to do mindfulness exercises. We strive to allow women to explore the magic of yoga and to learn to take time for themselves of their busy schedule. We believe that self-care is beneficial not only for mothers but also for those around them, including their children.

Inclusivity

We believe that diversity is a strength, and that inclusivity is essential to creating a welcoming environment. Every member of Femme Unity should feel valued, respected, and included, regardless of their ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other characteristic. We strive to ensure our community members have equal access to our classes and services and have the same opportunities for support. We actively incorporate inclusivity in our decision-making and in designing our services.
