Mom Yoga

Join our Kundalini class, designed for mothers seeking a transformative yoga experience. Connect with other like-minded individuals in a safe and inclusive space while prioritizing self-care. Awaken your Kundalini energy through physical exercise, meditation, and breathing techniques.
